# Industry-Ready ICT Tools

### 1. Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
- **Odoo (Community Edition):** Best for SMEs. Authentic and open-source.
- **ERPNext:** Highly recommended for manufacturing industries in Asia.

### 2. Monitoring & Control
- **Ignition SCADA:** The gold standard for modern industrial automation.
- **Grafana:** For real-time data visualization of sensor data.

### 3. Communication
- **MQTT Protocol:** The most reliable lightweight protocol for Industrial IoT.
- **Node-RED:** For wiring together hardware devices and APIs.
